Paris 1908

Here is the visualization for the year 1908. By default you can see the old map of Paris with the clusters of people. You can click on a marker to get the name of the corresponding person. Hovering over a cluster displays in blue its bound. Additional visualization are available:

  • the people displayed individually (not advised, as it is quite slow)
  • the density map (of both years, for comparison)
  • the arrondissements of Paris with the number of famous people within
  • the quarters of Paris with the number of famous people within
